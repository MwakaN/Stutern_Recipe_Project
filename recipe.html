Best Homemade Lasagna

About Lasagna

Lasagna, famed worldwide, has ancient origins and remains an Italian superstar dish. Basically, lasagna is a baked casserole made with wide flat pasta and layered with fillings such as ragú, bechamel, vegetables and various cheeses. Unsurprisingly, every Italian region has its own traditional recipe.

In southern Italy lasagna is generally made with dried sheets of pasta layered with rich meat ragú, ricotta and mozzarella. In the north, especially in Bologna, the most popular version of lasagna features fresh egg pasta colored green with spinach and layered with ragú, bechamel and Parmigiano Reggiano.

LASGNA INGREDIENTS:

INGREDIENTS QUANTITY
Round meat, (Italian sausage, beef, veal, lamb, or a mixture) 1 to 1-1/2lb
Ricotta cheese 1lb (0.45 kilograms
Mozzarella cheese 1lb (0.45 kilograms)
Egg 1
White onion - diced 1
Cloves garlic, crushed 2 tsp
Can crushed tomatoes 1 14 ounce
Tomato sauce 28 ounces
Tomato paste (optional) 6 ounces
Lasagna noodles (9-12 noodles) 1 box
Grated Parmesan or Romano cheese, to taste 1 tablespoon
Olive oil 11-2 tablespoons
Cheddar cheese (for topping) 2 cups

Steps/Method to Prepare Lasagna:

  1. Cook the noodles in boiling water.
  2. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a large pan on medium heat
  3. Cook the sauce on a low simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Add the 1lb of ground meat to the pan and cook until browned.
  5. Transfer the meat and vegetables to a large sauce pot on medium-low heat.
  6. Add the sauce and tomatoes to the saucepot and bring to a simmer.
  7. Whip your ricotta together with the beaten egg.
  8. Spoon a thin layer of sauce into the bottom of a large, oven-safe dish.
  9. Lay lasagna noodles over the entire bottom of the dish.
  10. Spoon 1/3 of the ricotta mixture evenly over the noodles.
  11. Spoon 1/3 of the sauce over the ricotta
  12. Top the sauce with a generous coating of mozzarella.
  13. Continue layering in this pattern --noodles, ricotta, sauce, mozzarella-- until you run out of noodles.
  14. Bake for 30-40 minutes at 375℉, covered with aluminum foil.
  15. Let the lasagna sit for 10 minutes before serving.
